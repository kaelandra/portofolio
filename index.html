Inside my world

Hi, i'm someone who lives somewhere between bursts of energy and quiet introspection, a classic INFP at heart. I find joy in the little things that stir emotion and imagination.

I enjoy writing stories and reading books. Both are ways i explore emotions, perspective, and the human condition. Speaking of that, i have a growing curiosity about psychology. I'm fascinated by how the mind works, what drives human behaviour, and how our thoughts shape who we are.

Music is a huge part of my life. I love playing guitar and losing myself in melodies that speak louder than words. When i'm not strumming strings, i'm probably watching drama series or anime, getting pulled into worlds that feel just as real as our own.

I play games to escape a little, to reset, and just have fun. There’s something about the rush, the teamwork, and the quiet focus it brings that i really enjoy. It’s not always about winning, sometimes it’s just about zoning out, clicking into the moment, and letting the world pause for a bit.
